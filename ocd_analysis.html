Compulsion score and Compulsion Type X Depression X Age interaction effect

Upon further analysis of demographic variables including age, gender, ethnicity, martial status, and education level, there was only a significant interaction between depression diagnosis and age (p = 0.030). Visualization indicated that among younger participants (<40), those without depression had slightly higher compulsion scores, whereas among older participants (>40), those with depression showed higher severity. This crossover suggests age moderates the relationship between depression and OCD compulsion severity.
